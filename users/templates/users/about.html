About Probashi Kallyan


Tradition since 1971

Bangladesh's number one income source is the foreign reserve. All the people including the government directly depend on foreign reserves. But our govt has no central application to manage or monitor them. For this reason, when they have gone to a foreign country, they face many problems. My system “Probashi Kallyan Management System” can solve all of this problem properly Such as job facility, living problem, salary, medical facility, passport renewal, visa problem, etc. Using this system, they can be benefited. If they face any problem in any foreign, they can get help from this system. Since no such facility has been introduced so far it is the first time in Bangladesh that would be made for our remittance fighters.
